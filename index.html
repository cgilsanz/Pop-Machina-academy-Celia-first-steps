Pop Machina Academy Week 4: Communities

what is a circular maker community ?

For me, a community is a group of people who share similar or complementary concerns and interests.

Therefore, a circular community is a community whose members have concerns related to the different concepts that encompass the circular economy, such as reuse, recycling, responsible consumption repair, responsible production and so on
