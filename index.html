Weekly Assignement

Week 2

In order to design the logo for the Circular Maker Space of Piraeus I used as reference the original logo of the Municipality

Since in many applications both logos are going to be used together I priotirised their visual balance along with maintaining the general image of the municipality. The original logo has a deformed circle as border which illustrates directly the idea of closed system.

Fonts where chosen in order to serve this idea of the circle and soft edges. Both fonts should accentuate the new area the municipality is entering as well as underline its historical importance throughout the years.
